About Us

Lifting Hands was founded on the basis of hurt. A lot of people in the world are going through a lot and the only way to help is to raise their voice loud. By raising their voice, they get funding from both within and outside borders. The goal at Lifting Hands is to final put a small on everyone faces. We help all areas of life such as community, religion, pets, medicals, etc. We are driven by supporting others.
